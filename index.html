About Ganga

Ganga might at first glance mostly be identified with the genre of “chill out music”. However, as in the words of the Artist of Ganga, Christian Ronn himself; "Musically, Ganga’s a chameleon.” For a man, who’s been making music for all corners of life and culture, this rings true. Ganga has been invested in a widely diverse spectrum of music, all from cinema film scores, to poetry.

When Ganga performs as a DJ, it is this melting pot of sounds and inspiration he brings to the show. With his over two decades of experience in the international musical circuit, Gangas bright, luscious, but rhythmically adventurous sound, continues to create that sought after atmosphere whose DNA is truly global.
